»»Fine Art Handling Services

Museum-grade operations.

Four surfaces. One operator. Miami-anchored crews and crating facility; beyond Miami, a vetted partner network. Every leg documented in WeMove.Art with operator attribution, so the move you book in Miami feels the same in São Paulo, Mexico City, or Basel.

01 — Global Transport·02 — Custom Crating·03 — Installation & Removal·04 — Storage Solutions
01

Global Transport

We are Miami-anchored and partner-extended. Local moves run on our own crews and vehicles; longer legs are executed through a vetted network of art-handling services we've worked with for years. Every shipment is assessed individually — air, road, sea, consolidated or exclusive-use — and routed for the right balance of speed, climate, and cost.

One coordinator owns the move end to end. You never hand off to a stranger, and you never lose visibility: each leg is logged in WeMove.Art with operator attribution, so you can see exactly who has the work, where, and when.

02

Custom Crating

Our full in-house woodworking facility builds to museum-level specification — any size, any geometry, from a single panel painting to multi-piece fair build cycles. Crates are engineered around the object: cavity packing, isolation against shock and vibration, and climate buffering sized to the destination and the season.

All export crating meets ISPM-15 and IPPC standards, so work crosses borders without a hold at customs. Designs are documented and archived, which means a recurring exhibition or a returning loan can reuse a proven crate.

03

Installation & Removal

Our handlers are museum-quality-trained and work white-glove on residential, commercial, and institutional sites. Every project is consultant-assessed before a crate is opened — placement, sightlines, lighting, wall construction, anchoring, and the condition of the work all reviewed on site.

We hang, mount, plinth, and rig — from a single work over a fireplace to a full gallery rotation — and we remove and re-pack with the same discipline. Condition is photographed before and after, time-stamped, and filed to the work's record.

Storage Solutions

Climate-controlled, security-monitored storage at our Miami HQ, with natural-disaster planning built into the building and the protocol. Short-term transit storage through to permanent collection holding, plus partner facilities worldwide when a collection lives in more than one place.

Everything is indexed to a rack-level location in WeMove.Art, so a work can be found, pulled, and scheduled for retrieval in minutes — same-day across the Miami metro. You see your inventory, its location, and its last condition check from anywhere.

»»Concierge Collection Management

Museum standards.
Private client services.

Three tiers. One philosophy.

Whether you're cataloguing an inherited collection or overseeing a multi-site legacy, our service tiers adapt to your needs without compromising museum standards. Excellence. Discretion. Insight.

Tier I

Foundation

For early-stage collectors and families organizing inherited collections.

Best for collectors with fewer than 25 works. The focus is to organize and protect the collection's physical and documentary integrity — a clean, complete, defensible record from the start.

"We inherited the work and the responsibility. They gave us the record we never had."

Inquire about Foundation »
  • » Professional inventory and barcoding
  • » Digital and printed catalogue with secure private access
  • » Initial condition assessments with photo documentation
  • » Discreet artwork tagging to verify presence and prevent loss
  • » Guidance on framing, placement, and conservation needs
  • » Annual report summarizing collection health
  • » Insurance advisory and basic valuation support
  • » Limited handling, transport, and installation coordination

Tier II · Most chosen

Signature

For mid-size or growing collections with significant financial and cultural value.

For collectors with multiple residences or active acquisition patterns. Ongoing oversight, strategic guidance, and broader risk mitigation across the whole collection.

"I'm acquiring across two cities. They keep the whole picture so I don't have to."

Inquire about Signature »

Everything in Foundation, plus

  • » Quarterly condition monitoring and movement verification
  • » Real-time alert setup for movement, absence, or environmental risks
  • » Quarterly appraisal cycle management
  • » Review and optimization of insurance coverage
  • » Unlimited strategic consulting sessions per year
  • » Advisory on acquisition, resale, and secondary market positioning
  • » Full coordination of storage, transport, and art-handler relationships
  • » Private event or exhibition planning and coordination

Tier III

Legacy

For high-value, generational, or institutional collections.

End-to-end stewardship for collections of 75+ works or significant multi-site holdings. Legacy integrates legacy planning, scientific imaging, museum-quality documentation, and global oversight into one continuous practice.

Advanced oversight & monitoring

Multi-site monitoring, real-time location reporting, environmental sensors for humidity, temperature, and shock.

Scientific & archival excellence

Annual scientific imaging cycle (UV/IR/X-ray as needed); full archiving of provenance, curatorial essays, and restoration records.

Strategic legacy planning

Coordination with trustees, heirs, and legal, philanthropic, and tax advisors.

Institutional & cultural engagement

Museum loan negotiation and donor placement support.

Priority client services

VIP priority for all logistics and emergencies.

»»About

Since 1987. Setting the standard for fine art services in Miami.

ArtMoves was founded in 1987. We are Miami's longest-running comprehensive fine art handling and collection management company. Our work is rooted in trust, tailored to your needs, and built to protect the cultural assets that define your legacy.

Mission

To set the benchmark in fine art logistics — combining unparalleled expertise with technology to deliver museum-grade services that redefine industry standards.

Vision

To empower and enrich the local art community by providing unparalleled, museum-grade logistics and support.

»»The team

Fernand Valery Adrien

Founder & CEO

Founder, operator, and architect of WeMove.Art. Fernand stewards every engagement personally — from intake to install — and built the operating platform the rest of the team runs on.

His conviction is simple: the people who own irreplaceable work deserve to see what the handlers see. That conviction became the company's technology, and the standard the team holds to.

Gaël Monnin

Director of Collection Management

Owner and curator of Galerie Monnin, descended from a family of gallerists that opened the gallery to the public in Haïti in 1956. Executive director of the Monnin Private Collection and an Emerson College graduate.

She worked in sales for the Delacroix family's fine art gallery in New York and facilitated the Lowe Art Museum's acquisition of a work by a major Haitian artist.
